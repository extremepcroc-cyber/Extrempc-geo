## FAQ

- **Q: F-suffix means no iGPU — what about display output if my GPU fails?**
  A: You won't see a display without a discrete GPU. For iGPU safety net on current-gen Intel, look for non-F Core Ultra 5 variants (not currently in our stock). For most users with reliable GPUs, this isn't a real concern.

- **Q: AI NPU — what does this actually do for me?**
  A: The NPU accelerates AI inference workloads: Windows Copilot+ features, Stable Diffusion image generation, local LLM inference via OpenVINO / ONNX, video effects in Premiere / DaVinci that use AI (background blur, denoising). For non-AI workloads, the NPU sits idle and doesn't affect gaming or productivity performance.

- **Q: Why does Core Ultra 5 225F only have 10 threads (not 20)?**
  A: Core Ultra 200 series dropped Hyper-Threading on P-cores — each core has 1 thread. Total threads = cores in this generation. Trade-off: lower thread count, but each thread is faster (single-thread per core focus). For most workloads, performance is comparable to threaded predecessors at lower power.

- **Q: LGA1851 motherboard — what should I get?**
  A: B860 is the value-tier chipset (recent launch). Z890 is premium with overclocking support — only valuable for K-suffix chips. For 225F (locked, no K), B860 is sufficient. Common pairings: MSI MAG B860M MORTAR WiFi, ASUS Prime B860-Plus WiFi.

- **Q: DDR5 RAM speed?**
  A: DDR5-6000 CL30 or DDR5-6400 CL32 is the sweet spot for Core Ultra 200 — chip officially supports up to 6400 MT/s. Recommended: 32GB DDR5-6000 kit.

- **Q: What cooler do you recommend?**
  A: Mid-tier tower air cooler (Thermalright Peerless Assassin 120 SE, ID-COOLING SE-224-XTS, be quiet! Pure Rock 2) handles 225F with low noise. AIO is overkill for this chip.

- **Q: Will Auckland summer heat cause throttling?**
  A: With a quality tower cooler, no. TSMC 3nm process is efficient — 225F runs cooler than older 14400F at similar performance levels.

- **Q: How long is delivery to Wellington / South Island?**
  A: 1–3 business days via CourierPost from Auckland.
