## FAQ

- **Q: Retail box vs OEM tray — what's the difference?**
  A: Same chip silicon, same Intel warranty, same MPN family. Retail box has the printed Intel product box; OEM tray ships in a plain protective tray. Choose retail if you value packaging or plan to resell later; choose OEM for pure value.

- **Q: Does the retail box include a cooler?**
  A: No. F-variant chips (including 14700F) do not include a cooler in either retail or OEM packaging. Only the lower-tier non-F locked chips include Intel stock coolers.

- **Q: Was 14700F affected by i7 degradation issues?**
  A: Partially. Intel issued microcode patches mid-2024 for i7-13700K and i7-14700K (unlocked-K variants drawing high power). 14700F is the locked-F variant with lower max power and benefits from the same microcode fix. Current shipping silicon has the fix applied.

- **Q: What cooler do I need?**
  A: Premium-tier tower air or 240mm AIO. Thermalright Phantom Spirit 120 EVO, Noctua NH-D15, or Arctic Liquid Freezer III 240. Skip entry-tier coolers.

- **Q: DDR4 or DDR5?**
  A: DDR5-5600 is the better match for the chip's productivity potential. DDR4-3200 works but limits multi-threaded throughput. For new builds, DDR5; for existing DDR4 reuse, still performs well.

- **Q: Which motherboard chipset?**
  A: Z790 recommended for 14700F — robust VRMs for sustained 219W turbo. Common pairings: ASUS TUF Gaming Z790-Plus WiFi, MSI MAG Z790 Tomahawk WiFi.
