## FAQ

- **Q: Is 750W enough for RTX 4070 Ti Super + Core i7-14700K?**
  A: Core i7-14700K has 253W PL2. RTX 4070 Ti Super has 285W TGP. Peak combined ~538W. 750W provides ~210W headroom above peak draw — sufficient. For Core i9-14900K (253W PL2, higher burst), 850W provides better margin.

- **Q: Does the 12VHPWR connector on ATX 3.1 eliminate adapter risks?**
  A: Yes — the documented melting issue with RTX 4090 connectors was traced to improper insertion of 8-pin→12VHPWR adapters and aftermarket cables. A native ATX 3.1 12VHPWR connector bypasses the adapter entirely.
