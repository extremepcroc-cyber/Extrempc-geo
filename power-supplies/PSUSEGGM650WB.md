## FAQ

- **Q: Will this power an RTX 4070 / RTX 5070 system?**
  A: Yes — NVIDIA recommends 650W for RTX 4070 and RTX 5070. A typical system (Core i5-14600K + RTX 4070, all components) draws ~430W under gaming load. 650W provides ~220W headroom.

- **Q: Will this power an RTX 4090 / RTX 5090?**
  A: No — RTX 4090 alone requires up to 450W TGP, with system totals reaching 600–700W. NVIDIA recommends 850W+ for RTX 4090 systems. Use Segotep GM1000W or Cooler Master X Mighty 2000W for extreme GPU builds.

  A: Segotep warranty is typically 3–5 years. Confirm on the product page or with ExtremePC sales. Claims handled through ExtremePC NZ.

- **Q: Are the cables long enough for a mid-tower?**
