## FAQ

- **Q: What is ATX 3.1 and why does it matter?**
  A: ATX 3.1 is the current PSU specification from Intel (2023). Key additions over ATX 2.x: (1) native 12VHPWR (16-pin) connector for RTX 40/50 series GPUs — no adapter needed; (2) improved transient load response for GPU power spikes. For RTX 4000/5000 series builds, ATX 3.1 eliminates the adapter-related cable risks documented on RTX 4090 installs.

- **Q: Will this run an RTX 4070 / RTX 5070 without an adapter?**
  A: Yes — KG650W includes a native 16-pin 12VHPWR connector. Plug directly into RTX 4070 / 5070 without any 8-pin to 12VHPWR adapter.

- **Q: White cables included?**
  A: Thermalright KG650W ships with white sleeved cables to match the white build aesthetic.

- **Q: Is 650W enough for RTX 4070 Ti Super?**
