## FAQ

- **Q: PALLAS II Black vs Vesta II RGB Black at the same NZD — which?**
  A: Same chip and speed. PALLAS II is non-RGB lower-profile (43.75mm) cleaner aesthetic. Vesta II RGB Black is taller (~50mm) with programmable RGB. For showcase builds, Vesta II. For non-RGB minimalist or builds with tight cooler clearance, PALLAS II.

- **Q: AMD EXPO + Intel XMP 3.0 — which to enable?**
  A: On AMD AM5: EXPO. On Intel LGA1700-DDR5 / LGA1851: XMP 3.0. Both profiles factory-tested.

- **Q: Black aesthetic — what builds does it match?**
  A: Dark-themed gaming builds (red/black, blue/black, all-black), productivity workstations where RGB is unwanted, professional content creator setups.

- **Q: AM5 sweet spot — why 6000?**
  A: AMD Ryzen 7000 / 9000 Infinity Fabric syncs 1:1 with memory up to ~2000 MHz. DDR5-6000 keeps FCLK at 2000 in sync — beyond 6000, FCLK desyncs which costs latency.

- **Q: Can I overclock beyond 6000 CL36?**
  A: Possibly with manual BIOS tuning. Hand-selected ICs validated for headroom to 8200 MT/s on supported motherboards. Stability silicon-dependent.

- **Q: Will it fit under my CPU air cooler?**
  A: Yes — 43.75mm height fits comfortably under Noctua NH-D15, Thermalright Phantom Spirit 120 EVO, Be Quiet Dark Rock 4. Vesta II RGB (~50mm with lightbar) is the variant where clearance may matter.

- **Q: Mixing this with my existing DDR5 stick — safe?**
  A: Strongly recommended not to mix different DDR5 kits or sticks. Buy matched 2× 16GB kit (this product) for guaranteed dual-channel operation at advertised speed.
