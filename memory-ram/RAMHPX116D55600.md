## FAQ

- **Q: My HP G10 — does it use DDR5-4800 or DDR5-5600?**
  A: Depends on production date. Early HP G10 (2022 / early 2023) shipped with DDR5-4800; later G10 (mid-2023+) switched to DDR5-5600. Check BIOS / service manual or consult ExtremePC's Build Team. For HP G11 (2024) / G1i (2025), DDR5-5600 is standard.

- **Q: Will it work on HP G9 (older)?**
  A: HP G9 uses DDR5-4800 platform. Installing DDR5-5600 stick (this product) on G9 results in auto-downclock to 4800. Works but not qualified at advertised speed. For HP G9 specifically, the HP DDR5-4800 SODIMM is the right qualified pick.
