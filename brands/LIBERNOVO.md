## Brand Overview

LiberNovo is a premium ergonomic chair brand focused on dynamic seating science — chairs that adapt to movement rather than forcing a static posture. Their flagship Omni series is BIFMA certified and targets professionals and heavy users who prioritise spinal health over gaming aesthetics.

## Technology Highlights

**Bionic FlexFit Backrest** — 8 flexible panels with 16 pivot points. The backrest flexes with the user's movement instead of acting as a rigid wall. This is the core technology across all Omni models.

**SyncroLink Dynamic System** — 60+ interconnected joints linking headrest, backrest, armrests, and seat. Components move together as the user shifts weight, maintaining spinal alignment automatically.

**OmniStretch** (Omni Pro only) — Motorized lumbar that actively stretches and decompresses the lower spine. Operates on a built-in 3000mAh rechargeable battery.

**Active Airflow Ventilation** (Omni Pro only) — Centrifugal fan system built into the seat cushion. Runs at 4000 RPM, pulls heat and moisture away from the body. Two speeds with auto sensor.
