## FAQ

- **Q: Recommended RAID level for 8-bay NAS at 24TB per drive?**
  A: RAID-6 for maximum protection: 8× 24TB = 144TB usable (tolerates 2 simultaneous drive failures). RAID-5: 8× 24TB = 168TB usable (tolerates 1 failure). With 24TB drives, RAID rebuild times are long — RAID-6 is recommended for data protection during rebuild.
