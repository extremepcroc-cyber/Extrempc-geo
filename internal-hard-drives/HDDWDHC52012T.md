## FAQ

- **Q: Ultrastar vs IronWolf Pro — which for my NAS?**
  A: IronWolf Pro has AgileArray firmware tuned for NAS bays (vibration compensation for adjacent drives). Ultrastar is tuned for rack JBOD where drives are vibration-isolated. For Synology/QNAP NAS, IronWolf Pro is the correct choice. Ultrastar in NAS is compatible but not optimal.

- **Q: AFR <0.35% — what does that mean?**
  A: Annualized Failure Rate below 0.35%. For comparison: typical desktop drives are 1–5% AFR. Enterprise Ultrastar drives are more reliable, meaning fewer drive failures per year across a fleet.
