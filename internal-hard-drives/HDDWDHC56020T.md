## FAQ

- **Q: Is Ultrastar the same as HGST?**
  A: Yes — WD acquired HGST in 2012. Ultrastar is the continuation of HGST's enterprise HDD line under the WD brand. The Ultrastar design lineage is HGST's enterprise engineering.

- **Q: HC560 vs HC520 — key differences?**
  A: HC560 20TB: 512MB cache, 20TB. HC520 12TB: 256MB cache, 12TB. Both share 2.5M MTBF, 550 TB/year, 5-year warranty, and AFR <0.35%.

- **Q: Can I use HC560 in a NAS?**
  A: Technically compatible, but Ultrastar lacks the NAS-specific vibration compensation (AgileArray) of IronWolf Pro. For Synology/QNAP NAS, IronWolf Pro or Synology HAT. For enterprise JBOD/DAS/SAN, Ultrastar.
