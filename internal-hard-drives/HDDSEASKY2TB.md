## FAQ

- **Q: How many days of recording does 2TB give?**
  A: Depends on cameras, resolution, and bitrate. Approximate guide:
  - 4 cameras × 1080p × 15fps H.264 (≈1 Mbps per camera): ~30 days
  - 8 cameras × 1080p × 25fps H.264 (≈2 Mbps per camera): ~10 days
  - 4 cameras × 4MP × 25fps H.265: ~14 days
  Most NVR software has a built-in storage calculator.

- **Q: Can I use this in a computer desktop?**
  A: Yes — it's a SATA drive and physically compatible with any desktop bay. But you're paying for surveillance firmware at 3× the desktop workload rating. For desktop use, BarraCuda 4TB at similar NZD offers 2× the capacity.

- **Q: Will it work with my Hikvision / Dahua / Reolink NVR?**
  A: Yes — SkyHawk is compatible with all major NVR brands. Check your NVR's compatibility list for confirmation.
