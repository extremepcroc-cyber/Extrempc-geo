## FAQ

- **Q: Is 8TB IronWolf compatible with Synology DS220+?**
  A: Yes — IronWolf 8TB is on Synology's official compatibility list for DS220+, DS223, DS420+, DS720+, and all current Synology models.

- **Q: RAID-1 vs JBOD for 2-bay NAS — which should I use?**
  A: RAID-1 (mirrored) for data safety: 8TB usable from 2× 8TB, with automatic drive failure protection. JBOD: 16TB usable but no redundancy. For home NAS, RAID-1 with regular backups is the standard recommendation.

- **Q: Does 7200 RPM mean higher heat / noise?**
  A: Marginally. 7200 RPM runs slightly warmer and louder than 5400 RPM. In a well-ventilated NAS enclosure (Synology DS220+ has dual fans), temperature is managed. For a quiet home office, the noise difference is minimal at 1 meter.

- **Q: Can I mix IronWolf 4TB and 8TB in the same NAS?**
  A: Yes, but RAID-1 pairs must be the same size (usable capacity = smallest drive × 2). RAID-5/6 can mix sizes but usable capacity = smallest drive × (N-1 or N-2).
