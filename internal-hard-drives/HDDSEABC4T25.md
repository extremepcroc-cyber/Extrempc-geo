## FAQ

- **Q: Will 15mm fit my laptop?**
  A: Most modern slim laptops (2020+) don't have any 2.5" bay at all (switched to M.2 only). Older laptops with 2.5" bays are typically 9.5mm depth. This drive is 15mm — only fits specific models with extra clearance. Measure before ordering or consult Build Team.

- **Q: Does this fit PS4 / PS4 Pro?**
  A: PS4 uses a 9.5mm 2.5" SATA drive. At 15mm, this drive does NOT fit.

- **Q: Can I use this in a USB enclosure?**
  A: Yes — standard USB 3.0 enclosures designed for 2.5" drives (7mm/9.5mm) may not accommodate 15mm height. Look for enclosures specifically supporting 15mm drives, or use an open-frame dock.

- **Q: 2-year warranty — is that adequate?**
  A: For secondary storage with regular backups, yes. For primary working drive, consider NVMe SSD with 5-year warranty.
