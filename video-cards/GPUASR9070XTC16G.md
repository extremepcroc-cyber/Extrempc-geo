## FAQ

- **Q: AMD vs NVIDIA at this tier — which?**
  A: For pure 1440p / 4K raster gaming + 16GB VRAM at meaningfully lower NZD with no PSU upgrade requirements, RX 9070 XT. For ray tracing-heavy or path-traced titles (Cyberpunk path tracing, Alan Wake 2 RT, Indiana Jones path tracing), DLSS 4-supported games, or CUDA-based AI workloads, RTX 5070 Ti. Both are excellent at this tier — match to your use case.

- **Q: What PSU do I need?**
  A: 800W minimum (quality), 850W 80+ Gold recommended for sustained max load. Standard 2× 8-pin connectors. Quality choices: Corsair RM850x, be quiet! Pure Power 12 M 850W, Seasonic Focus GX-850. No 16-pin or ATX 3.0 required.

- **Q: How does FSR 4 compare to DLSS 4?**
  A: FSR 4 (launched 2025) uses ML-based upscaling for significantly better image quality than FSR 3. Close to DLSS 4 Super Resolution quality. AMD's equivalent to DLSS 4 Multi Frame Generation is in development but not yet released. For most titles, FSR 4 is excellent; for Multi Frame Generation specifically, NVIDIA leads.

- **Q: Will it fit my case?**
  A: At 290mm length and 2.8-slot, fits all standard mid-tower and full-tower cases. Verify case max GPU length spec (typically 290mm+ needed). Compact SFF cases will not fit.

- **Q: Auckland summer heat with the dual 8-pin power and 304W TDP?**
  A: Triple-fan cooler handles sustained load in NZ ambient. For 28°C+ ambient or constrained case airflow, consider Steel Legend variant with premium components.

- **Q: How does RX 9070 XT do at 4K?**
  A: 4K ultra native: 60–80 fps in modern AAA. 4K maxed + ray tracing native: 30–45 fps. With FSR 4 upscaling: 4K + ray tracing reaches 80–100 fps in supported titles. For 4K maxed native without upscaling, step up to RTX 5080.

- **Q: Stable Diffusion / AI workloads on AMD?**
  A: AMD's ROCm (HIP) support for AI is improving but trails CUDA. For Stable Diffusion, AMD requires DirectML / ZLUDA / ROCm setup which has more friction than CUDA on NVIDIA. If AI workloads are a priority, RTX 5070 Ti / 5080 are the smoother experience.

- **Q: Linux gaming and content creation?**
  A: Excellent. AMDGPU + Mesa drivers ship with most Linux distros (Ubuntu, Bazzite, SteamOS, Pop!_OS). Steam Proton + AMD = smooth experience. Blender Cycles HIP (AMD's CUDA equivalent) is supported, performance is good but trails NVIDIA OptiX.

- **Q: How long is delivery to Wellington / South Island?**
  A: 1–3 business days via insured CourierPost from Auckland.
