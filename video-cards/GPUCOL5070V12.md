## FAQ

- **Q: Is the LCD screen useful or just gimmicky?**
  A: Depends on your build. For showcase builds with side-panel glass, the LCD adds genuine visual interest and useful info display (temps, FPS). For closed-panel cases or builds the user doesn't look at, it's wasted. Software (Colorful iGame Center) lets you customize what's displayed.

- **Q: Can I customize what the LCD displays?**
  A: Yes. Default shows GPU temp and clock; customizable to display custom images, GIFs, system stats, or off entirely.

- **Q: Will it fit in my case?**
  A: Vulcan is a larger 3-slot card. Verify case clearance — most mid-tower and full-tower cases accept it. SFF cases generally don't.

- **Q: Does the LCD screen ever break or need maintenance?**
  A: Magnetic mount means it's easily removable for cleaning. No moving parts in the LCD itself. Same 3-year warranty applies to the LCD as the GPU.

- **Q: How does the Colorful warranty work for the LCD specifically?**
  A: Covered under the same 3-year warranty through ExtremePC NZ. LCD failure (if it occurred) would be a warranty RMA.

- **Q: Is the bundled support stand actually necessary?**
  A: For 250W+ cards in standard horizontal mounts, yes — long-term vertical sag is a real issue. The stand keeps the card level over years of use.

- **Q: What PSU do I need?**
  A: 650W minimum (quality), 750W 80+ Gold recommended. Native 16-pin (12V-2x6) PSU cable preferred; adapter available.

- **Q: How loud is the cooler under load?**
