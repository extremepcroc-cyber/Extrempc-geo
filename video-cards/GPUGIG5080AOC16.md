## FAQ

- **Q: Will RTX 5080 handle 4K maxed + path tracing without DLSS?**
  A: In modern AAA at 4K ultra + ray tracing: 50–70 fps native. At 4K ultra + path tracing (Cyberpunk, Alan Wake 2 with path tracing): 30–40 fps native, becomes playable with DLSS 4. For absolute 4K maxed including path tracing native: step up to RTX 5090.

- **Q: What CPU should I pair with this?**
  A: AMD Ryzen 7 7800X3D for pure gaming (best 1% lows), Ryzen 9 9900X / 9950X for mixed productivity, Intel Core i7-14700F or Core Ultra 7 265K for content creation. CPU bottleneck on weaker chips wastes the GPU.

- **Q: PSU requirements?**
  A: 850W minimum (quality, ATX 3.0 preferred), 1000W 80+ Gold recommended for sustained max load. Native 16-pin (12V-2x6) cable required. Quality choices: Corsair RM1000x, be quiet! Dark Power 13 1000W, Seasonic Vertex GX-1000.

- **Q: Will it fit in my case?**
  A: AERO OC is a 3-fan, 3-slot card — typically 320mm+ length. Most mid-tower and full-tower cases accommodate it. Verify your case's max GPU length specification.

- **Q: How does AERO white aesthetic look in practice?**
  A: White/silver shroud with subtle RGB accents on side and front. Cleaner than full-RGB cards like the Strix series. Suits white showcase builds without overwhelming the visual theme.

- **Q: Stable Diffusion / AI workloads with 16GB?**
