## FAQ

- **Q: Why PRIME instead of TUF or Strix tier?**
  A: PRIME is ASUS's clean, no-RGB tier focused on engineering. TUF adds RGB and tougher armor; Strix adds aggressive aesthetics and premium VRMs. For users wanting flagship engineering features without RGB / aesthetic premium, PRIME is the value pick.

- **Q: How does the phase-change thermal pad help?**
  A: Phase-change material is the same thermal interface technology used on high-end CPUs and server GPUs. Outperforms standard paste under sustained load (5+ year lifespan, no pump-out, lower thermal resistance). Translates to lower GPU temps under long gaming sessions.

- **Q: Will it fit my SFF case (Fractal Terra / Lian Li Q58)?**
  A: Yes — 2.5-slot design is one of the slimmest RTX 5070 cards. Fits Fractal Terra (max 322mm), Lian Li Q58 (max 320mm), and similar compact builds.

- **Q: What's the difference between the Dual BIOS Performance and Quiet modes?**
  A: Performance mode runs maximum boost behavior with more aggressive fan curve. Quiet mode reduces fan speed for lower noise; boost behavior backs off slightly to maintain temps. Switch via hardware toggle on the card.

- **Q: 12GB VRAM — enough for 1440p ultra?**
  A: Yes for current AAA at 1440p ultra. 12GB on a 192-bit bus is the right balance for the chipset. For 4K native ultra or creative AI workloads, step up to RTX 5070 Ti or 5080.

- **Q: What PSU do I need?**
  A: 650W minimum (high-quality), 750W 80+ Gold recommended. Corsair RM750x, be quiet! Pure Power 12 M 750W. 16-pin (12V-2x6) PSU cable or included adapter required.

- **Q: Will my older PSU's adapter work?**
  A: ASUS includes a 2×8-pin to 16-pin adapter. Older non-ATX 3.0 PSUs (pre-2023) need the adapter; modern ATX 3.0 PSUs typically have native 16-pin cables for cleaner appearance.
