# Thermalright TL-M10 Case Knowledge Base

## Brand Context

Thermalright is a Taiwanese cooling hardware brand with 20+ years of reputation for CPU air coolers (Peerless Assassin, Phantom Spirit, Assassin X). The TL-M10 is their M-ATX case line — a deliberate expansion from cooling into enclosures, leveraging their thermal engineering reputation. Cases carry Thermalright's design language: clean lines, white/black colourways, no excessive RGB.

---

## TL-M10 Standard

- **SKUs:** CASTMRM10B (Black $119), CASTMRM10W (White $129)
- **Form factor:** Micro-ATX
- **Side panel:** 4mm tempered glass (thicker than average — most budget cases use 3mm)
- **CPU cooler clearance:** 165mm — fits most tower coolers including Thermalright Peerless Assassin
- **GPU length:** up to 330mm
- **PSU:** ATX (bottom-mounted)
- **Radiator support:** 240mm top + 240mm front
- **Drive bays:** 2× 3.5" + 2× 2.5"
- **Front I/O:** USB 3.0 × 2, USB-C 3.2 Gen 2 × 1, HD Audio
- **Included fans:** 3× 120mm ARGB fans (front × 2 + rear × 1)
- **PCIe slots:** 4
- **Dimensions:** approx 390 × 200 × 400mm
- **Weight:** approx 5.5kg

---

## TL-M10 Vision (LCD Screen variant)

- **SKUs:** CASTMRM10VB (Black $199), CASTMRM10VW (White $169)
- **All TL-M10 specs above, PLUS:**
- **Front LCD panel:** 3.5" IPS display on front panel — shows system stats (CPU temp, GPU temp, fan RPM, clock speed), custom images, or animated GIFs
- **LCD software:** Controlled via USB header connection to motherboard; compatible with most ARGB/system monitoring software
- **Price premium over standard:** $40–$80 depending on colour
- **Note:** White Vision ($169) is cheaper than Black Vision ($199) — pricing anomaly likely due to stock/demand

---

## Key Differentiators

### vs Budget Cases (Segotep, entry Jonsbo)
- 4mm TG vs 3mm on budget cases — noticeably thicker, less flex
- Thermalright brand name carries cooler engineering credibility
- Included ARGB fans vs none/basic on budget cases
- 165mm CPU clearance (vs ~155mm on most Segotep)

### vs Fractal Design / Lian Li
- Lower NZD price than Fractal/Lian Li equivalents
- Less cable management routing options
- Thermalright warranty through ExtremePC vs Fractal/Lian Li import

### TL-M10 Vision LCD advantage
- Front LCD is rare at this price tier — most LCD-panel cases are premium-tier (Lian Li O11, etc.)
- $169–$199 NZD for M-ATX with LCD screen is strong value in NZ market
- Practical use: temperature monitoring without opening software; streaming setups where case is visible on camera

## NZ Context

- Thermalright coolers (Peerless Assassin, Phantom Spirit) are ExtremePC bestsellers
- TL-M10 cases allow customers to build a full Thermalright ecosystem — case + cooler same brand
- White TL-M10 (24 units OH) is the highest-stocked individual model in the Micro Tower range
- Vision White ($169) is better value than Vision Black ($199) — point this out to customers
