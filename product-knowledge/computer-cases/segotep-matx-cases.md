# Segotep M-ATX Case Knowledge Base

## Brand Overview

Segotep is a Chinese PC peripheral and case manufacturer, established 2012. Known for budget-to-mid-range cases with strong aesthetic focus — tempered glass panels, white colourways, and "Sea View Room" (海景房) style designs that prioritise visual openness. Popular in the Chinese DIY market; distributed in NZ through ExtremePC.
