## FAQ

**Q: Are the included ARGB fans controllable?**
Yes — the fans use standard ARGB headers. Connect to motherboard 5V ARGB headers (or an included ARGB controller if provided) to control colours via BIOS or motherboard software (ASUS Aura Sync, Gigabyte RGB Fusion, MSI Mystic Light, ASRock Polychrome).

**Q: What does 4mm TG feel like vs 3mm?**
Noticeably thicker — the panel has less flex when removing or replacing it, produces less vibration rattle over time, and feels higher quality. The difference is subtle during normal use but evident during installation and maintenance.

**Q: Will a 240mm AIO fit in the front?**
