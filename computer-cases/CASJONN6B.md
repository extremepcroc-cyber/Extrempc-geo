## FAQ

**Q: What NAS operating systems work with the N6?**
Any NAS OS that runs on standard x86 hardware: TrueNAS Scale, TrueNAS Core, Unraid, Open Media Vault, Proxmox with ZFS, and others. M-ATX board compatibility means hardware selection is unrestricted.

**Q: Does it support SAS drives as well as SATA?**
SATA is the standard connection. SAS drive support requires an HBA card with SAS ports — the M-ATX PCIe slots in the N6 provide room for an HBA. SAS drives in SATA bays are not directly compatible without an adapter.

**Q: How many M.2 NVMe drives can I add?**
M.2 slots come from the motherboard, not the case. An M-ATX board with 2–3 M.2 slots allows NVMe cache (Unraid array cache) or boot drives alongside the 6× 3.5" HDDs.

**Q: Is the N6 quiet enough for a living room or home office?**
6× spinning HDDs produce audible low-frequency hum. The N6 is not a silent case — for living room placement, vibration isolation feet and acoustic foam placement help. It's more appropriate for a utility room, office closet, or home lab rack.

**Q: Does it include fans for HDD cooling?**
Check the product page for included fan configuration. NAS cases typically include at least 1–2 rear exhaust fans; additional intake fans for HDD airflow may be needed for high-density configurations.
