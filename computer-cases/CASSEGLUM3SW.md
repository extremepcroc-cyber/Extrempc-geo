## FAQ

**Q: What's the difference between Lumi 3S and Lumi 3T?**
3S = curved glass joint (smooth arc transition from side to top). 3T = flat glass joint (angular transition, more geometric look). Internal specs are identical. Choose based on the glass style you prefer.

**Q: Is the top glass permanent or removable?**
Typically the top glass panel is removable for fan/radiator access or if top airflow/intake configuration is preferred. Check the product page for the specific panel removal mechanism.

**Q: Does it support a 240mm AIO on the top?**
Top glass panel cases often have a top 240mm radiator mount accessible when the glass is removed. Confirm with the product page — AIO top mounting is feasible in many Sea View style cases.

**Q: Will the curved glass scratch easily?**
Tempered glass is scratch-resistant but not scratch-proof. Use a soft cloth for cleaning; avoid abrasive cleaners. The curved forming process doesn't reduce glass hardness.

**Q: Are RGB fans included?**
No fans included. For maximum RGB visual impact, budget for 2–3 ARGB 120mm fans for the front/top positions. Segotep, DeepCool, or budget ARGB fan sets from ExtremePC start at $15–$25 for a pack.
