## FAQ

**Q: Is the solid front panel removable for better airflow?**
Many Jonsbo solid front panels are removable to expose the fan mounting underneath. Check the product page for the front panel removal mechanism — this may allow converting the STD to an open-front configuration if thermal needs change.

**Q: Does the solid front affect temperatures noticeably?**
Solid vs mesh fronts can produce 2–5°C GPU/CPU temperature differences under sustained load with equivalent fans. In light-to-moderate use (office, casual gaming), the difference is negligible. For sustained high TDP workloads (rendering, heavy overclocking), a mesh front is preferable — see the D41 Mesh at $119.
