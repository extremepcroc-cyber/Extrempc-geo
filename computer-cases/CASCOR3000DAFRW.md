## FAQ

**Q: Are the included SP120 RGB fans iCUE compatible?**
Yes — Corsair SP120 RGB fans connect to the iCUE ecosystem via a Corsair RGB hub or directly to a Corsair Lighting Node. Full iCUE software control for colours, effects, and sync with other Corsair iCUE components.

**Q: Do I need Corsair hardware to use iCUE?**
iCUE software is free; only iCUE-compatible components can be managed through it. Non-Corsair ARGB components connect to motherboard headers and use the motherboard's own RGB software independently.

**Q: Does the 3000D Airflow support a 360mm AIO in the front?**
Yes — 360mm front radiator support is confirmed. The Corsair Hydro H150i Elite (360mm) fits in the front. Note that installing a 360mm AIO typically replaces the 3× included fans in the front position.

**Q: How does the 3000D Airflow compare to the standard 3000D?**
The 3000D Airflow has a mesh front for higher airflow at the cost of a less polished front appearance. The standard 3000D has a solid or glass front. For thermal performance, Airflow wins; for visual cleanliness, standard 3000D.
