## FAQ

**Q: Can I fit a 240mm AIO in the T9?**
No — the T9 has no radiator mounting points. Air cooling only, with a 70mm maximum cooler height. Noctua NH-L9i chromax, Thermalright AXP90-X47, and Noctua NH-L12S (with fan positioned on top) are appropriate choices.

**Q: What GPU length fits?**
Maximum 200mm. Check your specific GPU model's dimensions before purchasing — "mini" or "compact" variants from ASUS, Zotac, Gigabyte, and MSI are typically 170–200mm. Founders Edition and most triple-fan cards exceed 200mm and won't fit.

**Q: Does the all-aluminum body help with cooling?**
Aluminum has higher thermal conductivity than steel, contributing slightly to passive heat dissipation. The primary thermal management still comes from active airflow (case fans and CPU cooler). The contribution is real but not dramatic in a 10L chassis — the T9's benefit is aesthetic durability, not a cooling transformation.

**Q: Can I use a full-size ATX PSU?**
No — SFX only. The T9's internal volume doesn't accommodate ATX PSU dimensions. Seasonic Focus SGX, Corsair SF series, or SilverStone SX series are appropriate.

**Q: Is this suitable as a daily workstation PC?**
Yes — the T9's component support (ITX board, 200mm GPU, 70mm air cooler, SFX PSU) allows a fully capable workstation build. Running a Ryzen 5 7600 or i5-13600K with an RTX 4060 compact variant produces a machine capable of 3D rendering, video editing, and productivity work in a 10L package. M.2 NVMe storage only — factor in a fast NVMe drive for OS and project files.
