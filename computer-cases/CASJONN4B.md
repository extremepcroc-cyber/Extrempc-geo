## FAQ

**Q: If I use M-ATX, does the full-height PCIe slot become active?**
Yes — in M-ATX configuration, the case exposes a full-height PCIe slot. With an ITX board, only a half-height bracket is accessible (same limitation as N2/N3).

**Q: Can I install an ATX PSU if I'm using an ITX board?**
Technically the ATX PSU bay is sized for M-ATX config, but the PSU mounts independently of board size. Check case dimensions against your specific ATX PSU before ordering — SFX is the safer bet for ITX builds.

**Q: Is 82mm enough for a NH-U9S?**
The NH-U9S is 125mm tall stock but can fit with fan relocated or a slimmer fan. The Noctua NH-L12S (70mm with fan on top) fits within 82mm. For full tower clearance on a budget, Thermalright AXP120-X67 (67mm) fits cleanly.

**Q: Why is the N4 cheaper than the N2 when it supports M-ATX?**
Fewer drive bays (4 vs 5 for N2, vs 8 for N3). Bay count drives NAS enclosure pricing more than board size support.

**Q: Does the N4 come in white?**
Yes — Jonsbo N4 White (CASJONN4W) is available at the same price.
