## FAQ

**Q: Is the curved glass fragile?**
Hyperboloid glass is tempered — the same base material as flat TG panels. The curved forming process doesn't reduce glass hardness. However, the curved shape means it's more difficult to replace if broken vs a standard flat TG panel of the same dimensions. Handle with care during installation.

**Q: Does the curved glass distort the view of components inside?**
The convex curve creates a slight magnification/fish-eye effect on components near the centre of the panel — a design-intentional effect that adds visual depth. Components appear slightly rounded in perspective, not distorted in a degrading way.

**Q: Are fans included?**
No fans included with the TK-2. Budget for 3× 120mm front intake + 1× 120/140mm rear exhaust. ARGB fans inside the curved glass create a distinctive visual effect through the convex panel.

**Q: Does the curved side panel interfere with tall coolers or RAM with heatspreaders?**
The side panel bows outward from the case, increasing internal clearance rather than reducing it. The 170mm CPU clearance is measured from the motherboard tray to the panel's inner maximum inward point — the curved glass provides more clearance at the centre than a flat panel would at the same mounting distance.
