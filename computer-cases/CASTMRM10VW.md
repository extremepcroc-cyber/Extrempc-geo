## FAQ

**Q: Why is Vision White cheaper than Vision Black?**
Manufacturing cost variations between finish treatments at Thermalright's production level result in different prices. The white Vision being cheaper is a pricing anomaly — both have identical specifications.

**Q: How is the LCD display configured?**
Via USB internal header to the motherboard. Configuration is done through bundled software that supports GIF uploads, custom static images, and system sensor monitoring overlays.

**Q: Does the white interior show fingerprints inside the case?**
White interior panels attract dust and fingerprint visibility more than black. Use gloves during installation to keep the interior clean. A light wipe with a microfibre cloth after initial build keeps it looking sharp.

**Q: Will my GPU fit? I have an RTX 4070 Super.**
RTX 4070 Super Founders Edition is 267mm — well within 330mm clearance. Most AIB RTX 4070 Super variants run 280–320mm, also within spec. Check your specific card's dimensions.

**Q: Is the LCD the same as the Vision Black?**
Yes — same 3.5" IPS panel, same connectivity, same software support. The only difference between Vision Black and Vision White is the case exterior colour.
