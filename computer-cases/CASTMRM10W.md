## FAQ

**Q: Does white show dust more than black?**
White interiors do show dust accumulation more visibly than dark interiors. With 3× ARGB fans producing airflow, dust buildup in the case is reduced relative to passive configurations — but front panel dust filters (if included) and periodic cleaning every 3–6 months are recommended for white build maintenance.

**Q: Are the 3 fans all ARGB?**
Yes — all three included fans are 120mm ARGB. They connect to standard 5V ARGB headers on the motherboard for software control.

**Q: Will a 240mm AIO liquid cooler fit?**
Check the product page for the specific radiator mounting positions. The TL-M10 supports 120mm/240mm configurations — confirm front radiator mount dimensions before purchasing an AIO. If AIO is the cooling plan, note that AIO fans typically replace 2 of the included ARGB fans in the front position.

**Q: Can I coordinate the ARGB fans with other Thermalright components?**
Yes — if using a Thermalright cooler with ARGB fans (like the Peerless Assassin 120 Black TRX versions), connect all ARGB fans to the same motherboard header for unified control through the motherboard's RGB software.

**Q: How does TL-M10 White compare to NZXT H5 Flow White?**
NZXT H5 Flow White is a premium mid-range M-ATX case with superior cable routing and airflow optimisation at a higher price tier. TL-M10 White offers better immediate value with included fans and 4mm TG for less; H5 Flow is worth the step-up for builders who prioritise cable management aesthetics and NZXT's refined interior layout.
