## FAQ

**Q: Does the Infinity Mirror require software to activate?**
The Infinity Mirror front panel connects via USB header for software control (Lian Li L-Connect) for effects and colours. It may also have a standalone button for cycling preset effects without software.

**Q: Can I turn the Infinity Mirror off?**
Yes — the ARGB in the front panel can be turned off via software or the button. The front panel appears as a standard dark case front when LEDs are off.

**Q: Is 20Gbps USB-C compatible with all USB-C devices?**
USB-C devices negotiate the fastest mutual speed — a 10Gbps drive in a 20Gbps port will run at 10Gbps. No compatibility loss; only speed benefit with devices that support 20Gbps.

**Q: Is there a white version?**
Check extremepc.co.nz for colour availability. At time of writing, Lancool 217 INF is in stock in black (Infinity). For white ATX premium, see Corsair 3000D Airflow RGB White ($229) or Valkyrie VK03 variants.

**Q: How does Lian Li compare to Corsair or Jonsbo in case quality?**
