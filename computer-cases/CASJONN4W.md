## FAQ

**Q: Is the white finish the same quality as the Black model?**
Yes — same powder-coat steel chassis, different colour. Build quality and material are identical between N4 Black and N4 White.

**Q: Can I run a GPU in the N4 White for a hybrid NAS/gaming build?**
Only in M-ATX configuration (full-height slot active). Maximum GPU length depends on drive cage positioning — check specific GPU length against the N4's internal layout before purchasing a dedicated gaming card. A half-height low-profile GPU fits in ITX config; a full-size gaming GPU is tight and may require drive bay compromise.

**Q: Is there a white version of the N3 (8-bay)?**
No — the N3 is available in Black only. The N4 White is the largest-capacity white Jonsbo NAS case currently available in NZ.

**Q: What SFX PSU do you recommend for an ITX build in the N4?**
Seasonic Focus SGX 500W or Corsair SF600 Gold are reliable starting points. For a low-power NAS CPU (N100, i3-N305), 450W is sufficient. For 4 HDDs + moderate CPU, 500W is a comfortable headroom target.
