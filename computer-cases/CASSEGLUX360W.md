## FAQ

**Q: What's the difference between acrylic and tempered glass panels?**
Acrylic is a clear plastic — lighter, cheaper, and more impact-resistant than glass. It scratches more easily with abrasive cleaners or sharp objects. Tempered glass is heavier, scratch-resistant, and feels more premium. For a budget build or first build, acrylic is a practical trade-off. For a long-term showcase build, TG is the better material.

**Q: Can I fit a 360mm AIO in the front?**
Yes — 360mm front radiator support is the LUX 360's headline feature. Fit a 360mm AIO in the front mount for maximum cooling on a CPU-intensive build.

**Q: Are there case fans included?**
No fans included at $89. Budget for 1× 120mm rear exhaust as a minimum; 3× 120mm front intake if not using an AIO.
