## FAQ

**Q: Does the mesh front collect dust quickly?**
Mesh fronts accumulate dust faster than solid panels — airflow is higher, so more particles are pulled in. A front dust filter significantly reduces this. Check the product page for included dust filter; most Jonsbo cases include one. Clean the filter every 1–3 months in NZ conditions.

**Q: How does the mesh look aesthetically?**
Mesh fronts have a more industrial/functional aesthetic compared to glass or brushed steel solid panels. Modern gaming mesh cases look intentional rather than bare — most have a fine mesh pattern with minimal visible branding. The D41 Mesh is visually clean.

**Q: Will a 360mm AIO fit with the mesh front?**
Yes — 360mm front radiator support is confirmed. Install the 360mm radiator + fans behind the mesh front panel for maximum AIO cooling with intake airflow.

**Q: Are fans included?**
No fans at $119. Budget for 3× 120mm front intake + 1× 120mm/140mm rear exhaust. If using a 360mm AIO, the AIO fans cover the front intake positions.

**Q: Is there a white version?**
Not currently in stock at ExtremePC. The D41 range is available in black; for white ATX, see Segotep or Valkyrie options.
