## FAQ

**Q: Is the weight reduced vs a comparable steel case?**
Aluminum has lower density than steel — an aluminum-frame case is lighter than an equivalent steel case of the same size. The BO400CG's weight is lower than most full-featured ATX steel cases, making desk movement easier.

**Q: Does the aluminum frame scratch easily?**
Anodized aluminum is more scratch-resistant than painted steel but can mark under hard impacts. Handling the BO400CG carefully during installation is recommended. A scratched aluminum frame can be buffed but not repainted like steel.

**Q: Will this fit standard ATX components?**
Yes — standard ATX motherboard, ATX PSU (bottom mounted), PCIe GPU. 380mm GPU clearance covers most current flagships. 170mm CPU clearance accommodates all standard tower coolers.

**Q: Can I remove the TG panels?**
Tempered glass panels in the aluminum frame are typically removable for component installation, sitting in the frame channels. Check the product page for the specific glass mounting mechanism.

**Q: Is the $579 price justified vs a $329 TK-4?**
If visual showcase features (curved glass, dual-angle visibility, 420mm GPU) are the priority, TK-4 delivers more for $250 less. If material quality and desk aesthetic coherence (aluminum frame, gray color, long-term investment) are the priority, BO400CG is the correct product regardless of price comparison. These are different product categories serving different buyer motivations.
