## FAQ

**Q: Will an ATX motherboard fit?**
No — Mini-ITX only (170 × 170mm max board size). M-ATX or ATX boards will not fit the N2. For M-ATX support, see the Jonsbo N4.

**Q: Can I use a standard ATX power supply?**
No — SFX or SFX-L only. ATX PSUs are too large for the N2's PSU bay.

**Q: What's the difference between the N2 Black and N2 White?**
Identical specs and dimensions — colour only. The White version (CASJONN2W) suits lighter desk setups or matching white builds.

**Q: Can I add a GPU for occasional gaming?**
The single PCIe slot is half-height — suitable for a low-profile display adapter or NIC, not a gaming GPU. The N2 is a dedicated NAS platform.

**Q: Is there room for a 120mm AIO cooler?**
No radiator mounting points. Low-profile air coolers only (max 56mm). For a NAS CPU like N100 or i3-N305, the stock cooler or a $30 low-profile unit is sufficient.
