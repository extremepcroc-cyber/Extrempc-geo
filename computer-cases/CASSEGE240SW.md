## FAQ

**Q: Does it support a 280mm radiator?**
The Endura 240S is rated for 240mm front and top. 280mm radiator support is not guaranteed — check the product page for confirmed radiator positions and dimensions before purchasing a 280mm AIO.

**Q: What AIO coolers are compatible?**
Any 240mm AIO from major brands (Thermalright Frozen Edge 240, DeepCool LS520, Corsair H100i) mounts in the front or top position. Confirm hose length and routing for your specific build.

**Q: Is USB-C front I/O full 10Gbps USB 3.2 Gen 2?**
At this price tier, front USB-C is typically USB 3.2 Gen 1 (5Gbps) rather than Gen 2. Adequate for device charging and data transfer; not optimal for high-speed external SSD use.

**Q: Does it include case fans?**
Typically no fans included at this price point. Budget for 2× 120mm front intake + 1× 120mm rear exhaust as a minimum ($30–$45 for budget fans). If installing an AIO, the AIO fans cover the front or top position.

**Q: Can I use a 155mm CPU air cooler in this case?**
Yes — 155mm is the maximum, so a 155mm cooler fits exactly. Leave 5mm buffer if possible; the Thermalright TL-M10 ($129) offers 165mm clearance if your preferred cooler is taller.
