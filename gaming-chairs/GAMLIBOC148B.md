## FAQ

- **Q: I'm 175cm — should I get the 45cm or 48cm seat depth?**
  A: 175cm is in the overlap zone. The deciding factor is inseam length, not total height. Sit fully back: there should be a 2–3 finger gap between the seat edge and back of your knee. If you can't test in person, lean to 48cm if inseam is above 80cm or you tend to sit "perched" in most chairs.

- **Q: What does "electric lumbar" actually mean in practice?**
  A: Small motor moves lumbar support up/down via armrest button. SE tier uses manual screw dial behind the chair; Omni tier lets you change during the day without reaching behind. Pro tier adds OmniStretch decompression cycle on top.

- **Q: Is the 48cm version heavier or harder to move than the 45cm?**
