## FAQ

- **Q: I'm 175cm — should I get the 45cm or 48cm seat depth?**
  A: 175cm is in the overlap zone. The deciding factor is inseam length, not total height. Sit fully back in the chair: there should be a 2–3 finger gap between the seat edge and the back of your knee. If you can't test in person, lean to 48cm if inseam is above 80cm or you tend to sit "perched" in most chairs.

- **Q: Why no electric lumbar — is this an "old generation" chair?**
  A: No. SE is in current production alongside Omni and Pro. The manual lumbar is a deliberate design choice for users who prefer mechanical reliability over electronic convenience. The screw-dial mechanism is precise and has no end-of-life — it lasts as long as the chair frame.

- **Q: Is manual lumbar adjustment annoying day-to-day?**
  A: Only if you change positions frequently. Most users set lumbar once for their spine and leave it. The dial sits behind the lower backrest; one-handed adjustment takes 5 seconds.

- **Q: The base is nylon instead of aluminum — does this matter for a heavier user?**
