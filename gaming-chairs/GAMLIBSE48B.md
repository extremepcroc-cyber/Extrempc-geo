- Taller users (170–186cm) who need correct seat depth to avoid pressure behind the knee

- Budget-conscious buyers making their first move from a gaming chair to genuine ergonomic seating

## Comparison
- vs LiberNovo Omni 48cm ($2,099): Omni adds electric motorized lumbar for on-the-fly adjustment — worth the $400 upgrade if you frequently change positions throughout the day.
- vs LiberNovo Omni Pro 48cm ($2,399): Omni Pro adds electric lumbar, OmniStretch, and active seat fan cooling. If you sit 6+ hours or run warm, the Pro is the better investment.
- vs Secretlab TITAN Evo XL (~$700–$900 NZD): Secretlab is $800 less and a strong gaming chair. Omni SE's Bionic FlexFit backrest dynamically adapts to movement where Secretlab's lumbar is static — a real ergonomic advantage for back health over time.
- vs LiberNovo Omni SE 45cm (GAMLIBSE45B): Same chair, different seat depth. Choose 48cm if height is above ~172cm.
