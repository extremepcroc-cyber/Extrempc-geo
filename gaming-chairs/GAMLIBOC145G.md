## FAQ

- **Q: What does "electric lumbar" actually mean in practice?**
  A: Small motor in the backrest moves lumbar support up/down via armrest button. SE tier uses manual screw dial behind the chair (set-once, forget); Omni tier lets you change during the day without reaching behind. Pro tier adds OmniStretch decompression cycle on top.

- **Q: Does the Space Grey colour show stains differently than darker fabrics?**
  A: Gabriel Atlantic fabric is OEKO-TEX certified, machine washable cover. Light fabrics naturally show coffee/wine stains more readily until washed; if high-spill-risk, Midnight Black hides marks better.

- **Q: How long does the lumbar motor battery last?**
  A: In typical use (a few button presses per day), recharging needed every few months. The chair functions fully without the lumbar motor — you'd just lose push-button adjustment until recharged.

- **Q: Is this chair too "ergonomic-looking" for a casual home setup?**
