## Comparison
- vs LiberNovo Omni Pro ($2,399): Omni Pro adds active seat ventilation fan and OmniStretch motorized lumbar stretch. If you run warm or sit 6+ hours daily, the $300 upgrade to Pro is worth it.
- vs LiberNovo Omni SE ($1,699): SE uses manual lumbar (no battery, no charging). Omni's electric lumbar allows on-the-fly adjustment without reaching behind the chair. Worth the $400 premium if you frequently change your lumbar setting.
- vs Secretlab TITAN Evo (~$700–$900 NZD): Secretlab offers a fixed lumbar pillow. Omni's Bionic FlexFit backrest dynamically adapts to movement — a better choice for users with posture or back concerns.
- vs LiberNovo Omni 45cm Space Grey (GAMLIBOC145G): Identical chair, colour only differs.
