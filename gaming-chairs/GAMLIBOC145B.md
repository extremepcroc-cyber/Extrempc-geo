## FAQ

- **Q: What does "electric lumbar" actually mean in practice?**
  A: There's a small motor in the backrest that moves the lumbar support up/down via push button on the armrest. On the SE entry tier, you manually turn a screw dial behind the chair — fine if you set it once and forget it. On the Omni tier, you can change lumbar position during the day (more forward when typing, less forward when reading) without reaching behind. The Pro tier adds a separate motorized decompression cycle (OmniStretch).

- **Q: How long does the lumbar motor battery last?**
  A: Battery specifics vary by build; in typical use (a few button presses per day), recharging is needed every few months. The chair functions fully without the lumbar motor — you'd just lose push-button adjustment until recharged.
