## Comparison
- vs Herman Miller Aeron (~$2,800–$4,500+ NZD): Aeron uses passive mesh, no active cooling, limited recline range. Omni Pro adds motorized lumbar stretch and active fan ventilation at a lower NZD price point. Herman Miller leads on warranty (12yr vs 5yr) and sustainability credentials.
- vs Secretlab TITAN Evo (~$700–$900 NZD): Secretlab uses a static lumbar pillow. Omni Pro's Bionic FlexFit backrest dynamically flexes with body movement. For users with back health concerns, Omni Pro is the stronger long-term investment.
- vs LiberNovo Omni ($2,099): Omni Pro adds active seat ventilation and motorized lumbar stretch. Worth the $300 premium for heavy daily users or warm climates.
