## FAQ

- **Q: 2TB vs 1TB — is the capacity upgrade worth it?**
  A: In 2025, yes for most gamers. Modern AAA games are 100GB+ each; 1TB fills with 8–10 games. 2TB gives 15–20 game headroom. NZD/GB at 2TB is meaningfully better than 1TB tier.

- **Q: DRAM-less at 2TB — does it matter more than at 1TB?**
  A: Slightly. Larger DRAM-less drives can show more random IOPS variance under heavy sustained load. For typical gaming + light content creation, real-world impact minimal. For heavy sustained writes (video render scratch, large file batches), DRAM cache (Samsung 990 PRO, Predator GM7) is meaningfully better.

- **Q: NV3 vs higher Kingston tiers (KC3000, Fury Renegade)?**
  A: NV3 is Kingston's value line. KC3000 and Fury Renegade are premium lines with DRAM, faster speeds, 5-year warranty, higher NZD. For mainstream value, NV3. For Kingston premium, KC3000 (not currently in our stock).

- **Q: Will it work in my motherboard?**
  A: Most motherboards from Intel 12th Gen+ or AMD Ryzen 5000+ have Gen4 M.2 slots. NV3 boots in Gen3 slots at Gen3 speeds.

- **Q: Heatsink — required?**
  A: Motherboard built-in M.2 heatsink recommended for Gen4 drives under sustained load. For Auckland summer ambient, the heatsink prevents thermal throttling.

- **Q: How long is delivery to Wellington / South Island?**
  A: 1–3 business days via insured CourierPost from Auckland.
