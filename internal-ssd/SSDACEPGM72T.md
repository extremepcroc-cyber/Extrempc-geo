## FAQ

- **Q: GM7 vs Samsung 990 PRO 2TB — which?**
  A: Performance is within 1–2% (essentially equivalent in real-world). 990 PRO has stronger brand reputation and longer reliability track record. GM7 has 5-year warranty matching 990 PRO at meaningfully lower NZD. For brand-priority builds, 990 PRO. For value-premium with equivalent specs, GM7.

- **Q: 7400 vs 7200 MB/s (GM7 vs GM6) — does the small difference matter?**
  A: Real-world gaming difference: marginal. The 5-year warranty is identical. NZ stock difference is the key (92 vs 35). For walk-in build deadlines, GM7's deeper stock matters more than the speed gap.

- **Q: DirectStorage support — what does this enable?**
  A: DirectStorage is a Windows API that lets games load assets directly from SSD to GPU, bypassing CPU. Supported in Forspoken, Ratchet & Clank Rift Apart, and a growing list of titles. Requires Gen4 NVMe SSD + RTX 30/40/50 or RX 6000+ GPU. GM7 is fully compatible.

- **Q: DRAM cache vs DRAM-less at 2TB — does it matter more at larger capacity?**
  A: Yes — larger drives benefit more from DRAM cache for random IOPS consistency under heavy load. GM7 (DRAM) maintains performance better than NV3 (DRAM-less) at 2TB under sustained workloads.

- **Q: 5-year warranty — what's covered?**
  A: Manufacturing defects and premature failures. Industry-standard premium SSD warranty length.

- **Q: Heatsink — required?**
  A: Strongly recommended for sustained Gen4 performance at 7400+ MB/s. Most current motherboards include built-in M.2 heatsinks; aftermarket if motherboard lacks one.

- **Q: How long is delivery to Wellington / South Island?**
