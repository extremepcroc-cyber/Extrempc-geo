## FAQ

- **Q: Samsung 990 PRO vs Samsung 990 PRO with Heatsink — which?**
  A: Samsung sells both. Without heatsink (this SKU) at lower NZD; rely on motherboard heatsink. With heatsink at higher NZD. Most current motherboards (Intel B760, Z790, AMD B650, X670) include built-in M.2 heatsinks — bare drive is fine. For builds without motherboard heatsink, with-heatsink version or aftermarket heatsink.

- **Q: 1,200 TBW endurance — what does this mean?**
  A: 1,200 TBW = 1.2 petabytes of writes over the drive's life before endurance warranty ends. Typical consumer use ~50 TBW/year = 24+ years. For most gaming + content creation users, the 5-year time limit ends warranty before endurance.

- **Q: 2TB at modern sweet spot pricing — is this the right capacity for 2025?**
  A: Yes for most gamers. Modern AAA games are 100GB+ each. 2TB holds 15–20 modern AAA games + OS + working files comfortably. 1TB fills in 6–8 games. 4TB is for users with extensive game libraries or content creation primary drive use.
