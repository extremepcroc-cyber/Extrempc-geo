- **Q: DRAM cache — does it matter?**
  A: Yes for sustained workloads. DRAM cache (this GM6 has it) maintains performance under heavy writes and random IOPS. DRAM-less drives (NV3, G50) perform similarly at peak sequential reads but slower under sustained load. For OS / primary drive, DRAM matters.
