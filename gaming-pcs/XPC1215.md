## FAQ

- **Q: Is the Ryzen 9 9900X worth it over the 9700X for gaming alone?**
  A: For pure gaming at 1440p, not really — the RTX 5070 is the limiting factor, not the CPU. In most titles at 1440p, the 9900X and 9700X are within 3–5%. The 9900X pays off when you're doing productivity work alongside gaming.

- **Q: Can this handle streaming while gaming?**
  A: Yes — the 12 cores give you plenty of headroom for game + OBS + browser + Discord. The RTX 5070's NVENC encoder handles stream quality. You won't see frame drops during streams.

- **Q: Is 1TB enough storage?**
  A: For 15–20 modern AAA titles, yes. If you have a larger library or work with large video files, consider the 2TB upgrade at checkout or add a secondary drive later.

- **Q: Does this come with Windows 11?**

- **Q: How does the 9900X compare to Intel's i7-14700K?**
  A: The 9900X is more power-efficient (TSMC 4nm vs Intel 7) and runs significantly cooler under sustained loads. Gaming performance is within 2–4% at 1440p. The 9900X wins on efficiency and thermals; the 14700K has a slight edge in some single-threaded productivity benchmarks.

- **Q: What monitor should I pair with this?**
  A: A 27" 1440p 165Hz IPS panel is the natural pairing. The RTX 5070 will drive this resolution at high settings comfortably. Going to 32" 4K is possible but the GPU will struggle with Ultra settings — 1440p is the sweet spot.
