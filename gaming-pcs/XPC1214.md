- **Q: Is 64GB RAM overkill for gaming?**
  A: Yes, for gaming alone — 32GB is more than enough for any game in 2026. The 64GB pays off when you're doing productivity work alongside gaming: video editing, VMs, large datasets, or heavy multitasking. If you only game, the XPC1215 (32GB) is the smarter buy.

- **Q: Is the Ryzen 9 9900X worth it over the 9700X for gaming alone?**
  A: For pure gaming at 1440p, not really — the RTX 5070 is the limiting factor, not the CPU. In most titles at 1440p, the 9900X and 9700X are within 3–5%. The 9900X pays off when you're doing productivity work alongside gaming.
